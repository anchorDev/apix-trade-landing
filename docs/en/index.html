Frequently Asked Questions

For Traders

What Functions Can The APIX.TRADE Platform Copy? APIX.TRADE

Our platform copies all the functions that can be carried out on a standard trading exchange, including the ability to hedge.

How Do You Connect An Investor?

In the trader's account, in the account settings, you will find your link for registering new investors. Also, if you wish, you can change the name of your link. A new investor can follow that link to use your services.

How Will My Investors Pay For My Services?

After profits are calculated by our intelligent systems, your investors will be invoiced for payment. To pay the invoice, the investor needs to replenish the internal balance by transferring USDT (BEP2) according to the details specified in his personal account.

How Can I Withdraw Profit to My Wallet?

Yes. After calculating the profit, after the invoices for your services have been paid by your investors, the funds will be credited to your internal account in the APIX.TRADE system. To withdraw funds, you need to go to the homepage of the trader's account, click on "Internal Balance", enter your details: address and MEMO (USDT.BEP2), and send a withdrawal request. Within 24 hours your withdrawal request will be processed, in the "Payment History" you can see the link to the transaction on the blockchain.

I Have an Open Position on the Exchange, a New Investor Has Connected to Me, Will He Open This Position?

If the current PNL of your (their) open position is negative, then the system will copy this position to a new investor at a "better" price. If the current PNL of your open position(s) is positive, the system will ignore that position until you close it, or until the PNL becomes negative.

For Investor

I Added API Keys, How Do I Start Copying?

After adding API keys to your account, you need to click on the "START" button located at the top of the homepage of your account.

I Added API Keys, Clicked the Start Button, but APIX Still Asks Me to Add Them Again. Why?

Most likely you did not grant rights to "Universal Operations". Delete your old API key, create a new one, and add it again to your personal account.

Can I Withdraw or Fund My Account at the Time of Open Transactions?

If you close your account or some of your investments, then our platform will automatically reduce part of your position to equalize the percentage. And in case of replenishment, there will be an automatic increase in the position.

How to Top Up Your Internal Balance?

Click on the internal balance button on the homepage of your account. A pop-up window with the USDT BEP2 address will appear. Send the required amount to this address. When replenishing, be sure to indicate the MEMO.

How Much Profit Is Calculated?

The calculation of profit always comes from the amount that you currently have on your balance sheet. It takes into account the profit you received from transactions that were previously made.
